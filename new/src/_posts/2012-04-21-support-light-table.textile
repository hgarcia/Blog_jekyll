Light Table is a concept for a new IDE introduce by Chris Ganger and inspired by this video of Bret Victor - Inventing on Principle.

Chris "introduced the project in a post on his blog":http://www.chris-granger.com/2012/04/12/light-table---a-new-ide-concept/ and due to the "great reception":http://www.chris-granger.com/2012/04/15/light-tables-numbers/ by the community he decided to submit the project to "Kickstarter":http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/ibdknox/light-table to get the founds needed to make it a real product.
